Fashion Nest - FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers to help you out.

How can I track my order?
You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. Alternatively, use the tracking link sent to your registered email.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Please ensure the product is in its original condition with all tags intact. Visit our Return Policy page for detailed information.
How can I contact customer support?
You can contact us through the "Contact Us" page or email us at support@fashionnest.com. Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you.
Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?
Yes, you can change your delivery address before the order is shipped. Go to the "My Orders" section and edit your address. For shipped orders, contact our customer support.
